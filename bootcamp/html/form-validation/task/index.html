# Form validation - exercises 1. Create a form which will ask for following fields: - username - email address - phone number - comment (multiline text) 2. Add following validation: - make all form controls mandatory - give the username field a required length between 5 and 20 characters - give the phone field maximum length of 15 characters - give the comment field maximum length of 100 characters - usernames should consist of a single lowercase letter, followed by a dot, and the rest any of lowercase letters
